A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Denton, Texas, with my partner and 2 precocious dogs. My journey into tech started with a career in Speech-Language Pathology. As I began to delve deeper into the field, I realized not only how much was lacking in terms of access and accessibility for the populations I served, but also the inefficiency and cumbersome nature of computer healthcare documentation systems.

That curiosity opened the door, and when I began to learn how to code I realized that I found something that gave me not only freedom, but also the versatility that I have been looking for in work. Now I am looking for a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organizational skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
